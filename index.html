Train in
Paradise

Located at the Zanzibar Sporting Club in Kiwengwa, Fit Pit is the East Coast's premier fitness destination. Whether you're a local resident, a hotel worker, or a traveller looking to keep up your routine — we've got you covered.

With modern equipment, expert trainers, group classes, and exclusive women's-only hours, Fit Pit caters to every fitness level in a welcoming, supportive environment.
